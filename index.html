About me

I started coding in college while I was studying vocal performance and electronic music. My first big project was building a laser midi harp that I could interface with my computer and play any instrument loaded in my data base. An unfortunate accident forced me to take a break from music and I started to apply skils in the tech industry. I have worked for some of the industries hottest startups including Square, Groupon, Luxe, Fundbox, and Handy.

Skills

HTML 5, CSS 3, Javascript, Node.js, Angular.js, APIs, JSON, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Bootstrap, Express, Mongoose, MongoDB, Github, Jira Admin, Agile Framework, bcrypt PostgreSQL, Rspec, SalesForce Admin, Desk Admin, Zendesk Admin.
